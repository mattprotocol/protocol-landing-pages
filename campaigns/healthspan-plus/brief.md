# Campaign Brief: Healthspan Plus

## Concept
Outcomes-focused landing page for Protocol's standalone remote PCP + longevity medicine membership. Positions Healthspan Plus as the physician relationship health-optimizers actually want — data-driven, proactive, and comprehensive — at a fraction of traditional concierge medicine pricing.

## Target Audience
**Primary:** Health-optimizers (biohacker-adjacent) — already tracking metrics, doing bloodwork, interested in longevity. Want a doctor who speaks their language and goes deeper than standard care.

**Secondary:** High-income professionals — busy execs/founders who want concierge-level care without the $20K+/yr concierge price tag. Time, access, and outcomes matter more than in-person face time.

**Psychographics:** Proactive, data-literate, willing to invest in health. Frustrated by 15-minute doctor visits and surface-level bloodwork. Already spending on supplements, testing, and fitness — but lack medical coordination.

## Primary Value Proposition
A dedicated remote PCP who practices proactive, longevity-focused medicine — backed by advanced diagnostics, coaching, and a full care team — for $749/month. Everything a $27K+ concierge setup offers, coordinated under one roof.

## Key Messages
1. "Your doctor should know your VO2 Max" — provocative signal that this isn't standard healthcare
2. The math: $27K+/yr à la carte vs $7,995/yr all-inclusive
3. Not telemedicine — a real physician relationship, delivered remotely
4. Evidence-based, PubMed-cited protocols — not proprietary supplement stacks
5. Full care team with defined roles — physician-led, not coach-led

## Objections to Address
- "Is this just telemedicine?" → Dedicated PCP relationship, not random doctor visits
- "$749/month is expensive" → 70% less than assembling this yourself; compare to concierge $15-25K
- "I already have a doctor" → Most PCPs don't have time for proactive optimization
- "Is this legit/evidence-based?" → PubMed citations, peer-reviewed protocols, real outcomes
- "What do I actually get?" → Clear benefits section with 6 specific inclusions
- "What happens after I sign up?" → 30-day onboarding timeline
