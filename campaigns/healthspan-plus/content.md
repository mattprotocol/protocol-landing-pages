## What Is Healthspan+?

Healthspan+ is Protocol's remote longevity medicine program -- a physician-led medical team, health coach, and registered dietitian building evidence-based protocols for health optimization, delivered entirely via telehealth. No zip code required.

### The Problem with Standard Healthcare

The healthcare system was not built to optimize your health. It was built to treat disease after it arrives.

- **"Your labs are normal."** Normal compared to who? The average American? Normal range means you're not actively dying. It tells you nothing about where you could be.
- **Seven minutes, once a year.** That's what your doctor has. Seven minutes to understand your biology, your goals, your risks. It's not a care plan. It's a checkbox.
- **The standard of care is decades behind the science.** The research on VO2 max, ApoB, metabolic health, and early disease detection is clear. Most doctors don't test for it. Most don't know to.

### A Better Model of Care

There is a different way to practice medicine. One that doesn't wait for disease to arrive. One that uses the best available science to find risks early, pursue real health optimization, and keep you at your best. Now available wherever you are.

Healthspan+ brings you a dedicated longevity physician, a health coach, and a registered dietitian. One medical team, working from your data, building protocols designed for your biology.

### Your Medical Team

You're not hiring a doctor. You're gaining a medical team that sees the complete picture and acts on it.

- **Longevity Physician** -- Your dedicated longevity doctor specializes in proactive medicine and health optimization. They interpret your data against optimal ranges, build your medical protocol, and conduct quarterly reassessments as your biomarkers evolve.
- **Health Coach** -- Regular 1:1 sessions focused on exercise programming, sleep optimization, stress management, and sustainable habits. Your coach translates the physician's protocol into daily action.
- **Registered Dietitian** -- Nutrition strategy grounded in your lab data. Not generic meal plans. Targeted dietary interventions based on your biomarkers, metabolic profile, and health goals.
