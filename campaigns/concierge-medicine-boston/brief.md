# Campaign Brief: concierge-medicine-boston

## Concept
Google Ads landing page targeting "concierge medicine" and "concierge primary care" keywords in the Boston market. Content-rich page designed for high Quality Score with keyword-dense copy explaining what concierge medicine is and how Protocol's model works.

## Target Audience
Boston-area professionals 35-55 actively searching for concierge primary care. High intent — these searchers already know what concierge medicine is and are evaluating providers.

## Primary Value Proposition
Physician-led concierge primary care with 24/7 access, a full care team, and advanced diagnostics — not just longer appointments, but a fundamentally different model of care built on data and outcomes.

## Key Messages
1. Dedicated physician with 24/7 access — not a rotating door of doctors
2. Full care team (MD, PA, health coach, dietitian, care coordinators)
3. Advanced diagnostics with 60+ biomarkers, DEXA, VO2 max
4. Quarterly reassessments, not annual check-ups
5. Insurance billed for primary care; membership covers longevity optimization

## Objections to Address
- "Is this just telemedicine?" — No, in-person diagnostics at Boston partner facilities
- "Is it covered by insurance?" — Primary care billed to insurance, membership covers extras, HSA/FSA eligible
- "Is it worth the cost?" — Contrast 7-minute visits vs 60-minute visits, 10 biomarkers vs 60+
- "Can I keep my current PCP?" — Yes, Protocol focuses on proactive/longevity care
