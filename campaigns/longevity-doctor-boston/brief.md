## Target Audience
- Health-conscious professionals ages 30-65 in Greater Boston
- Frustrated with reactive "sick care" and superficial annual physicals
- High-income, proactive, willing to invest in prevention
- Searching for longevity doctors, clinics, or healthspan optimization

Protocol tests 60+ biomarkers where your annual physical tests 20. Physician-led longevity medicine that prevents disease before it starts, with quarterly tracking to measure real progress.

3. Data-driven protocols: DEXA, VO2 Max, advanced bloodwork, all calibrated to your biology
4. Physician-led with coaching: not a wellness app, real medicine with a real care team
5. Boston-based: local partner facilities for in-person diagnostics
