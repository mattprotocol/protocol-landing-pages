# VO2 Max Testing Boston - Campaign Brief

## Objective
Capture search demand for VO2 max and cardiorespiratory fitness testing in Boston via Google Ads.

## Target Keywords
- "vo2 max testing near me"
- "vo2 max test boston"
- "cardiopulmonary fitness test boston"
- "metabolic testing boston"

## Target Audience
- Fitness-focused adults aged 30-55 in Greater Boston
- Familiar with VO2 max from podcasts (Huberman, Attia) or fitness communities
- Willing to pay out-of-pocket for clinical-grade testing
- May be training for endurance events or optimizing general fitness

## Positioning
- Clinical-grade testing (medical metabolic cart, not gym-grade)
- Physician-interpreted results (not just a printout)
- Part of a larger longevity medicine practice (DEXA, bloodwork, etc.)
- Actionable output: personalized exercise prescription, not just a score

## Conversion Goal
Discovery call booking via GHL form modal.

## Differentiators vs. Competitors
- Gym/studio VO2 tests use submaximal estimates or consumer-grade equipment
- Protocol uses medical-grade metabolic cart with direct gas exchange
- Results interpreted by physician in context of full health picture
- Quarterly retesting cadence built into membership
