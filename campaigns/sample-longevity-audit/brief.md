# Campaign Brief: The Longevity Audit

## Concept
Standalone landing page for Protocol's Longevity Audit — a $1,700 comprehensive biological baseline assessment. The audit is both a standalone product and the entry point to Protocol's membership. This campaign validates the landing page system end-to-end.

## Target Audience
Health-conscious professionals aged 35-55 in Austin, TX who:
- Already invest in health (gym, supplements, wearables, Oura/Whoop)
- Want data-driven optimization, not generic advice
- Have disposable income ($1,700 is accessible but considered)
- Are proactive about health, not reactive
- Follow: Peter Attia, Andrew Huberman, Bryan Johnson, Levels Health

## Primary Value Proposition
You can't improve what you haven't measured. The Longevity Audit gives you a complete biological baseline so every health decision is calibrated to your numbers.

## Key Messages
1. Start with data, not guesswork — baseline before protocol
2. Comprehensive: bloodwork (ApoB, Lp(a)) + VO2 max + DEXA + medical review
3. Actionable: personalized protocol roadmap, not just a report
4. Evidence-based: every recommendation from peer-reviewed research
5. Most "healthy" people have 2-3 markers outside optimal they don't know about

## Objections to Address
- "Why not just get bloodwork from my doctor?" → We test ApoB, VO2 max, DEXA — markers most annual physicals skip
- "$1,700 is expensive" → Piecemeal equivalent: $500+ bloodwork + $200 DEXA + $300 VO2 + analysis = $1,000+ without the roadmap
- "I'm already healthy" → Most "healthy" people have 2-3 markers outside optimal range they don't know about
- "What do I do with the results?" → You get a protocol roadmap, not just numbers on a page
