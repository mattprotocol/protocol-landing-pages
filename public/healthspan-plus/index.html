Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

How does remote longevity medicine work?

Your longevity physician orders labs through a national lab network near you. Once results are in, you meet via video for a detailed review. Your health coach and dietitian work with you through regular video sessions. Quarterly physician check-ins track progress and adjust protocols. The medicine is the same. The delivery is remote.

What tests will I need to do locally?

Bloodwork is drawn at a lab near you. We coordinate the orders and send you to a Quest, Labcorp, or local lab. The draw takes about 15 minutes. If you want DEXA or VO2 max testing, we can recommend facilities in your area, though these are optional add-ons for remote members.

Do you accept insurance?

Healthspan+ operates outside of insurance. Insurance-driven medicine dictates what your doctor can test for and how long they can spend with you. Our model removes those constraints so we can practice medicine the way the science says it should be practiced. Some lab work may be covered by your insurance separately.

What is the commitment?

Healthspan+ is $749/month or $8,995 paid upfront, with a one-year commitment. Proactive medicine is a continuous process. Meaningful changes in biomarkers take months to manifest, and longitudinal tracking is where the real value lies. The annual commitment ensures your team can build, implement, and refine your protocol over time.

Is this evidence-based?

Everything we do is grounded in published, peer-reviewed science. We don't do unproven interventions, trendy supplements, or experimental protocols. Board-certified physicians. If the evidence isn't there, we don't do it.

How is this different from concierge medicine?

Concierge medicine gives you more time with a doctor who practices the same reactive model. A longevity doctor takes a fundamentally different approach. More access to the same playbook. Healthspan+ is built from the ground up around proactive, longevity-focused medicine. Different tests, different targets, different model of care entirely. Plus a health coach and dietitian working alongside your physician.

Does this replace my primary care doctor?

Healthspan+ focuses on proactive medicine and health optimization. We recommend keeping your local PCP for acute care, prescriptions, and in-person needs. Your longevity physician works alongside your existing care team, not in place of it. We coordinate and share findings as appropriate.
